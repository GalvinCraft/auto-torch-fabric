# AutoTorcher

**Do you forget to place torches while exploring caves? Or maybe you just want to make sure you're never in complete darkness? If so, AutoTorcher is the mod for you!**

Remove some tasks from your Minecraft adventures by letting AutoTorcher handle torch placement for you. With this mod installed, torches will be automatically placed whenever you find yourself in a light level of 0, ensuring you always have visibility in dark areas, and a mob-free experience.

Let yourself mine, or eat, or just explore without needing torches in your hand. Just make sure you have torches in your inventory though.

_A torch a day keeps the mobs away!_ Or something like that.

# How to use
Just keep torches in your inventory, and you'll find that the surrounding space will be automatically lit up!

# Supported versions
[![Supported versions](https://modrinth-shields.imgalvin.me/badge/autotorcher)](https://modrinth.com/mod/autotorcher)

AutoTorcher is available for Minecraft versions 1.21.10 and above!

Due to the frequency of Minecraft updates now, each Minecraft version has its own .jar file. This is mainly to prevent crashes from each drop.

Please also keep your loader and Fabric API up to date to ensure the best compatibility!

## 1.1.0 - 14/12/2025
Closes [#2](https://github.com/GalvinCraft/auto-torch-fabric/issues/2)

Gives players the ability to toggle AutoTorch functionality for themselves. New commands for this are

- `/autotorch on` - Turns AutoTorch on for the player
- `/autotorch off` - Turns AutoTorch off for the player
- `/autotorch toggle` - Toggles AutoTorch on or off for the player
- `/autotorch debug` - Enable debugging (admins only for servers, singleplayer requires cheats)
